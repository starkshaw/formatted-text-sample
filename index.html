I am a Cat

—Soseki NAT­SUME

“He is to­tally aban­doned in the way he buys book af­ter book, never to read a sin­gle one. I would­n’t mind if he used his head and bought in mod­er­a­tion, but no. When­ever the mood takes him, he am­bles off to the biggest book­shop in the city and brings back home as many books as chance to catch his fancy. Then, at the end of the month, he adopts an at­ti­tude of com­plete de­tach­ment.”
